## 1. Economic Implications: The Arbitrage Engine

*   **The Shift**: We stop being a *Consumer* of Cloud Services and become an *Arbitrager* of Compute.
*   **Mechanism**: By abstracting the substrate (L5 Node Layer), Indrajaal can bid dynamically for resources.
