## 2. Product Portfolio: The "Foundation Stack"

We target the 5 pillars required to build any web service, replacing centralized incumbents with our decentralized, verified alternatives.

### 2.1 Service A: Sovereign Identity (I2S-ID)
*   **Target**: Replaces Auth0, Okta, Firebase Auth.
*   **Capability**: Leveraging `Indrajaal.Safety.Guardian` and ICP Internet Identity.
*   **Value Prop**:
    *   No vendor lock-in (Identity owned by user/holon).
    *   Biometric (Passkey) by default.
    *   Cross-chain/Cross-system authentication (SSH, HTTPS, Canister calls).
    *   "Login with Indrajaal" - Zero-password, unphishable.
*   **Revenue Model**: Per-Active-User (MAU) or tiered License for Enterprise Holons.

### 2.2 Service B: Verifiable Audit Trails (I2S-Proof)
*   **Target**: Replaces Splunk (for compliance), Datadog (for logs), AWS CloudTrail.
*   **Capability**: Leveraging `ImmutableState` (DuckDB), `ZenohNeuralStream`, and ICP Chain Fusion.
*   **Value Prop**:
    *   **Cryptographic Finality**: Logs are hashed and anchored on-chain. Impossible to forge or delete (Compliance).
    *   **Fractal querying**: Query logs at Edge (fast) or Chain (verified) without data movement costs.
    *   **Legal-Grade**: Admissible as evidence due to signature chains.
*   **Revenue Model**: Storage Volume + Retention Period (paid in Cycles/Stablecoins).

### 2.3 Service C: Autonomic Security (I2S-Immune)
*   **Target**: Replaces Cloudflare WAF, CrowdStrike, legacy IDS/IPS.
*   **Capability**: Leveraging `Sentinel` (Detection), `Mara` (Red Teaming), and `Antibody` (Response).
*   **Value Prop**:
    *   **Active Defense**: Doesn't just alert; *neutralizes* threats via Antibodies.
    *   **Resilience Testing**: "Chaos-as-a-Service". Mara constantly tests the subscriber's app resilience.
    *   **Shared Immunity**: Threat signatures detected on one Holon protect ALL Holons instantly.
*   **Revenue Model**: Protection Tier (Traffic Volume) + Threat Intelligence Feed subscription.

### 2.4 Service D: Biomorphic Observability (I2S-Pulse)
*   **Target**: Replaces New Relic, Prometheus/Grafana (SaaS).
*   **Capability**: Leveraging `Prajna Cockpit`, `SmartMetrics`, and Fractal Telemetry.
*   **Value Prop**:
    *   **Health Score**: Not just raw metrics, but a "Biological Health Score" (0.0-1.0).
    *   **Prediction**: AI Copilot predicts failures *before* they happen (Pre-Error).
    *   **OODA Integration**: Metrics feed directly into decision loops, not just dashboards.
*   **Revenue Model**: Per-Host/Per-Holon monitoring fee.

### 2.5 Service E: Resilient Mesh Networking (I2S-Mesh)
*   **Target**: Replaces VPNs, Tailscale (commercial), AWS VPC Peering.
*   **Capability**: Leveraging Zenoh, WireGuard, and DHT Federation.
*   **Value Prop**:
    *   **Zero-Trust**: No trusted middleman.
    *   **Serverless P2P**: Direct Holon-to-Holon high-bandwidth pipes.
    *   **Resilience**: Survives internet fragmentation (Mesh routing).
*   **Revenue Model**: Bandwidth (if relayed) or Enterprise Connectivity License.
