## 🎯 Executive Summary

This comprehensive roadmap outlines the strategic development path for the STAMP (System-Theoretic Accident Model and Processes), TDG (Test-Driven Generation), and GDE (Goal-Driven Execution) integrated system within the Indrajaal Security Monitoring Platform. The roadmap spans 3 years and represents a $50M+ investment in next-generation software development methodologies.

### Strategic Vision
Transform Indrajaal into the world's premier example of safety-critical software development by integrating systematic accident prevention (STAMP), test-first AI development (TDG), and cybernetic goal execution (GDE) into a unified, enterprise-grade development ecosystem.
