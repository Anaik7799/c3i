## 6.0 Glossary of New Lexicon

*   **Autopoiesis**: A system capable of reproducing and maintaining itself.
*   **Active Inference**: A framework where the system acts to fulfill its own predictions about its healthy state.
*   **Holon**: A unit that is simultaneously a whole and a part (e.g., an Agent is a whole system to its workers, but a part of the Domain).
*   **Maxwell's Demon**: An intelligent filter in the Cockpit that selectively admits high-value information to reduce operator cognitive load.
*   **Proprioceptive Interface**: A dashboard that allows the operator to "feel" the system state (e.g., through entropy heatmaps) rather than just reading metrics.
*   **System DNA**: The cryptographic hash of the core safety axioms (`CLAUDE.md`), embedded in every runtime component to ensure compliance.
*   **Vickrey Auction**: A sealed-bid second-price auction used for internal resource allocation.
