**Comprehensive Training Program for Safety, Quality, and Goal Achievement**

## 🎯 Training Overview

This training center provides complete educational materials for mastering the STAMP/TDG/GDE framework - a revolutionary approach combining:

- **STAMP**: Systems-Theoretic Accident Model and Processes for safety
- **TDG**: Test-Driven Generation for quality assurance
- **GDE**: Goal-Directed Execution for systematic achievement

## 🎖️ Certification Program

### Certification Levels

#### 🥉 Practitioner Level
**Requirements:**
- Complete Foundation Track (16 hours)
- Pass foundation assessment (80%+)
- Submit 1 STPA analysis
- Demonstrate TDG compliance for 30 days
- Achieve 3 GDE goals

**Benefits:**
- Digital certificate
- Community access
- Basic mentorship

#### 🥈 Professional Level
**Requirements:**
- Complete Professional Track (32 hours)
- Pass professional competency test (85%+)
- Lead 3 STPA analyses
- Maintain 95%+ TDG compliance for 60 days
- Manage 10+ GDE goals successfully

**Benefits:**
- Advanced certification
- Speaking opportunities
- Mentor training
- Priority support

#### 🥇 Expert Level
**Requirements:**
- Complete Expert Track (40 hours)
- Pass expert certification exam (90%+)
- Conduct 5 CAST investigations
- Train 10+ developers in TDG
- Implement enterprise GDE system

**Benefits:**
- Master certification
- Conference presentations
- Curriculum development
- Advisory board invitation

### Certification Maintenance
- Annual recertification (8 hours continuing education)
- Community contributions (blog posts, presentations)
- Peer mentoring (minimum 2 developers per year)
- Framework improvements (suggestions and implementations)
