# Sales Email — Draft targeted sales outreach

Draft a sales email for "$ARGUMENTS":

## Rules
1. **Subject line**: Under 50 chars, no clickbait, curiosity or value-driven
2. **Opening**: Reference something specific about the recipient (role, company news, shared connection). NO "I hope this email finds you well"
3. **Body**: 
   - One pain point they likely have (inferred from their role/industry)
   - One concrete result you can deliver (with numbers if possible)
   - Maximum 3 short paragraphs, under 150 words total
4. **CTA**: Single, low-friction ask (15-min call, reply with thoughts, check a link)
5. **Tone**: Peer-to-peer, not salesy. Confident, not desperate. Helpful, not pushy.

## Variants
Generate 3 variants:
- **A — Direct**: Lead with the value prop
- **B — Curiosity**: Lead with a question about their challenge
- **C — Social proof**: Lead with a relevant customer result

## Follow-up Sequence
Draft a 3-touch follow-up cadence:
- **Day 3**: Short bump (2 sentences, new angle)
- **Day 7**: Value-add (share a relevant insight, article, or benchmark)
- **Day 14**: Break-up email (final attempt, respectful close)

## Anti-Patterns (NEVER do these)
- Generic "we help companies like yours" language
- Listing features instead of outcomes
- Asking for 30+ minutes on a first touch
- Using "just checking in" or "circling back"
- Wall of text (if they need to scroll, it's too long)
