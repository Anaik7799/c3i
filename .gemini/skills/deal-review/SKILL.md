## 3. Deal Velocity Analysis

## 4. Competitive Position

- Our strengths vs theirs (feature/value matrix)
- Landmines to set (questions that expose competitor weakness)
- Traps to avoid (areas where we're weak)

## 5. Champion Health Check

- Can champion sell internally without us in the room?
