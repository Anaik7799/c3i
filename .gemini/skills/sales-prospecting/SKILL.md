# Sales Prospecting — Research and qualify leads

Research and build a prospect profile for "$ARGUMENTS":

## 1. Company Intelligence
- Company name, industry, size, revenue range, HQ location
- Recent news, funding rounds, product launches, leadership changes
- Tech stack signals (job postings, BuiltWith, GitHub activity)
- Pain points inferred from public information (earnings calls, press releases, Glassdoor)

## 2. Contact Mapping
- Key decision-makers (C-suite, VP-level, Directors in relevant departments)
- Organizational structure and reporting lines (from LinkedIn data)
- Champions vs economic buyers vs technical evaluators
- Warm paths: mutual connections, shared events, common groups

## 3. Qualification (MEDDPICC Framework)
| Criteria | Finding |
|----------|---------|
| **M**etrics | What business outcomes do they measure? |
| **E**conomic Buyer | Who signs the check? |
| **D**ecision Criteria | How do they evaluate solutions? |
| **D**ecision Process | What's their buying process/timeline? |
| **P**aper Process | Legal, procurement, security review? |
| **I**mplicate Pain | What happens if they do nothing? |
| **C**hampion | Who internally advocates for us? |
| **C**ompetition | Who else are they evaluating? |

## 4. Outreach Strategy
- Personalized value proposition (3 bullet points max)
- Recommended channel: email, LinkedIn InMail, phone, warm intro
- Timing signals: fiscal year end, budget cycle, trigger events
- Suggested opening message (under 100 words, no fluff)

## 5. Account Score
Rate 1-10 on: Budget fit, Need urgency, Authority access, Timeline alignment
Composite = (B + N + A + T) / 4 — prioritize accounts >= 7.0

Output a structured account brief ready for CRM entry.
