## 6. Kill Criteria
If these are true, KILL the deal and reallocate effort:
- [ ] No champion identified after 3 attempts
- [ ] Economic buyer refuses meeting after 2 requests
- [ ] Customer says "not this fiscal year" or "budget frozen"
- [ ] Competitor has signed MSA/SOW
- [ ] Deal value doesn't justify effort (< threshold)

## 7. Verification Gate
- [ ] MEDDPICC scores based on evidence, not hope
- [ ] Constraint identified with root cause
- [ ] Acceleration plays are actionable (not vague)
- [ ] Kill criteria honestly evaluated
- [ ] 14-day plan has concrete actions, not "follow up"
