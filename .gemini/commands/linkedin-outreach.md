# LinkedIn Outreach — Craft LinkedIn messages and engagement strategy

Create a LinkedIn outreach strategy for "$ARGUMENTS":

## 1. Profile Optimization Check
Before outreach, verify your profile signals credibility:
- [ ] Headline = value prop (not just job title)
- [ ] Banner image aligned with professional brand
- [ ] About section tells a story with social proof
- [ ] Featured section has relevant content/case studies
- [ ] Recent activity shows thought leadership (not just likes)

## 2. Target Research
For each target contact:
- Current role, tenure, career trajectory
- Recent LinkedIn posts/articles (engagement hooks)
- Shared connections (warm intro paths)
- Groups, events, interests in common
- Content they engage with (topics, format)

## 3. Connection Request (300 char limit)
Draft 3 variants:
- **A — Mutual interest**: Reference shared group/interest/connection
- **B — Content-based**: Reference their recent post with genuine insight
- **C — Value-first**: Offer something useful (insight, intro, resource)

Rules:
- NEVER pitch in the connection request
- NEVER use "I'd love to pick your brain"
- NEVER send blank connection requests to cold prospects
- Keep under 280 chars (buffer for names)
- Be specific — generic = ignored

## 4. Post-Connection Sequence
| Day | Action | Content |
|-----|--------|---------|
| 0 | Connect | Personalized request (see above) |
| 1-3 | Engage | Like/comment on their recent content meaningfully |
| 3-5 | DM 1 | Thank for connecting + one relevant insight/question |
| 7-10 | DM 2 | Share value (article, benchmark, intro) related to their role |
| 14-21 | DM 3 | Soft ask: "Would it make sense to exchange ideas on [topic]?" |

## 5. Content Engagement Strategy
To build visibility before and during outreach:
- Comment thoughtfully on target's posts (add insight, not just "Great post!")
- Share content relevant to their industry/challenges
- Tag and mention appropriately (not spammy)
- Post your own POV content on topics they care about

## 6. Sales Navigator Filters (if available)
Recommended saved searches:
- By title + industry + company size + geography
- By recent job changes (new role = budget + mandate)
- By content engagement (active posters = responsive)
- By technology used (if relevant to your solution)

## 7. InMail Templates (if Sales Navigator)
Draft InMail for when connection request is ignored:
- Subject: Under 40 chars, value-driven
- Body: 3 sentences max — hook, value, CTA
- Personalize the first sentence with something ONLY they would relate to

## Anti-Patterns
- Mass connection requests with identical messages
- Pitching immediately after connecting
- Long messages (keep DMs under 100 words)
- "I see you're in [industry]" — too generic
- Automation tools that send at 3 AM their timezone
