## 6. Patterns & Anti-Patterns Discovered

### Patterns (use these)

1. **Warm intro via Infosys = 60% response rate** — the single most powerful conversion lever. Every Tier 1 target where Infosys has an existing account relationship should use this path.
2. **Event context = 20-30% response for cold targets** — "I'll be at GSA EEF and would value 15 minutes" performs 3-4x better than generic outreach. Use for targets where no warm path exists.
3. **Day 0 private dinner = highest conversion quality** — intimate setting with 8-10 people creates coach/champion relationships faster than any other format. Worth the €3-5K investment.
4. **GSA meeting platform = force multiplier** — if sponsoring at Gold level, GSA facilitates 1:1 meeting scheduling. This is worth the sponsorship cost alone.
5. **24-hour follow-up = momentum preservation** — response rates drop 50% after 72 hours post-event. S5 must be same-day or next-morning.

### Anti-Patterns (avoid these)

1. **No pre-event outreach** — arriving at EEF without confirmed meetings = relying on hallway collisions with 300 people
2. **Generic "let's connect" LinkedIn request** — must include specific value hook: "I noticed Nokia's 6G research centre announcement — InSemi's verification team has relevant experience I'd like to share"
3. **Same follow-up to everyone** — each S5 email must reference something specific from the S4 conversation
4. **No ZK capture during event** — if meeting notes aren't in ZK within 2 hours, details are lost. Use voice memo → transcribe → ZK ingest
5. **Stopping at S5** — the "thank you" email is not the goal. S6 (discovery call) is the minimum viable outcome.

## 13. Conclusion

The S0-S7 Prospect-to-Pipeline model fills the gap between "identified event target" and "qualified pipeline opportunity." It connects the existing contact relationship stages (cold→warm→coach→champion), outreach hierarchy (SOP-008), and design-win funnel into one end-to-end process.

For GSA EEF 2026: 40 identified targets → 15-20 confirmed meetings → 12-15 held → 5-8 discoveries → 3-5 pipeline entries at $2-5M weighted value.

The model is event-agnostic and can be reused for DVCon Europe (October 2026), Semicon Europa (November 2026), and any future industry events.

**Critical success factor**: S2 outreach must start by May 1 — 6 weeks before the event. Every week of delay reduces S3 confirmation rate by ~10%.
